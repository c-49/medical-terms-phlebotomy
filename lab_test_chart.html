üìù Important Reminders:

  • Patient Identification: Always verify patient identity with 2 identifiers before specimen collection
  • Tube Fill: Fill tubes to the appropriate level - especially critical for coagulation tests (light blue tubes)
  • Order of Draw: Blood culture ‚Üí Light Blue ‚Üí Red ‚Üí Green ‚Üí Lavender ‚Üí Gray
  • Fasting: When required, typically means 8-12 hours with only water allowed
  • Timing: Some tests are time-sensitive (cortisol, testosterone) - note collection time
  • Transport: Some specimens require special handling (ice, body temp, light protection)
  • Labeling: Label tubes at bedside immediately after collection
